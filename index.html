Mela Whole Foods Mobile Grocery

Mela is short for "Hlokomela," which translates to "take care."

Empowering communities through fresh, affordable, and high-quality food—delivered with heart, purpose, and a commitment to equity.

Our Mission See Our Impact

Mission Statement

Our mission is to create a thriving, community-centered grocery store that provides affordable, fresh, and high-quality food to areas where there are food deserts, senior citizens who are unable to access grocery stores, the disabled and low-income families. We are committed to bridging the gap in food access by offering nutritious options at prices that empower families to thrive. At the heart of our business is a deep dedication to fostering economic growth and sustainability, with a particular focus on promoting ownership and leadership within the food industry. Through community engagement, local partnerships, and a commitment to excellence, we aim to build a space where families feel valued, supported, and connected, all while creating opportunities for economic empowerment and long-term generational wealth for entrepreneurs.

Vision

To create a vibrant, thriving grocery store that serves as a cornerstone for economic stability in underserved communities. By making nutritious and affordable food accessible to all, we aim to empower individuals to lead healthier lives while fostering local prosperity. Our commitment to sustainability drives our operations, ensuring that every product we offer is sourced responsibly, with an emphasis on environmental stewardship. Above all, we champion entrepreneurs, providing a platform to showcase and uplift their businesses, thus building a more equitable and resilient economy for future generations.

Our Objectives

Accessible, Healthy, Affordable Groceries

We ensure every family, regardless of income, has access to nutritious, fresh, and affordable food. We offer healthy options, educational resources, and empower families to make informed choices for their well-being.

Empower the Local Community

We create sustainable jobs, prioritize local hiring, and supporting entrepreneurs. Our vision is to increase ownership in the food industry and build generational wealth through mentorship and economic mobility.

Promote Sustainability

We source locally, reduce waste, and operate with eco-friendly practices. We partner with other locally owned businesses and educate our community on sustainability for a healthier future.

Community Empowerment

We fill the gap in food deserts, build trust, and create economic stability in marginalized communities.

  • Support local farmers and suppliers for fresh, culturally relevant food.
  • Mentor and inspire entrepreneurship in the community.
  • Leverage community pride and support for lasting impact.
  • Offer alternative delivery models for families with mobility challenges.
  • Mobile markets, pop-up stores, and subscription boxes for rural and remote areas.
  • Partner with small-scale farmers and co-ops for local, sustainable products.
